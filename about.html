Our Story

Hi, I’m Giuliano — a maths tutor with over a decade of experience and a passion for helping students unlock their potential. My journey began while studying Mathematics at the University of East Anglia, where I first discovered the power of one-to-one teaching.

During that time, I worked with a charity that helped children with their schoolwork. This was where I first saw the life-changing impact that one-to-one teaching can have.

One of my first students was a quiet Year 9 boy who was failing every maths test. After six months of working together, he passed with flying colours.

Seeing his face light up when he got his results confirmed my mission — to help students discover what they are truly capable of.

In 2020, I started the GG Maths YouTube channel to help ambitious students prepare for Olympiad-level challenges and advanced problem-solving. Since then, the channel has grown to over 4,200 subscribers and 260,000+ views, reaching students all over the world.

Over the past ten years, that mission has continued to grow.

What started as just me tutoring has now become a small, hand-picked team of highly qualified maths tutors. Each one shares my passion for helping students succeed.

Today, GG Maths is a team of experts providing high-quality, personalised maths tuition to students all across the UK.
