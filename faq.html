Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to some of the most common questions from parents and students.

About Lessons

How are lessons delivered?

All lessons are delivered 1:1 online via Google Meet using interactive whiteboards, so your child can see worked examples in real-time and share their own solutions.

What subjects and levels do you teach?

We specialise in maths for ages 7–18, covering Primary, GCSE (Foundation & Higher), and A-Level, across all major UK exam boards.

Do you offer group lessons?

Our focus is on 1:1 tuition, but in certain cases we can arrange small group sessions for siblings or friends who are at a similar level.

How flexible is scheduling?

We’ll find a regular time that works for you, and if things change, we’re happy to reschedule when possible. Our goal is to make tutoring fit easily around school and family life.

Do you offer a trial lesson?

Yes — every student starts with a free trial lesson. This gives families a chance to meet the tutor, experience our style, and decide if it’s the right fit before committing.

What equipment does my child need?

All that’s required is a computer or tablet with internet access. We’ll guide you through setup and provide links to our resources. A headset or quiet workspace is recommended for the best experience.

Results & Approach

How quickly will my child improve?

Many students see a jump of 1–2 grades in just a few months, and 2–4 grades over a year. The exact progress depends on consistent attendance, practice, and engagement. We set clear milestones so you’ll always know how your child is improving.

What makes GG Maths different from other tutors?

We go beyond just “turning up and teaching.” Our tutors are hand-picked, highly qualified, and personally trained by Giuliano to deliver structured, engaging lessons. Every student gets a personalised plan, access to exclusive resources, and support that continues between lessons.

Can students message tutors between lessons?

Yes! If your child is stuck or needs help during the week, they can message their tutor and usually get a response within 24 hours. Support doesn’t stop when the lesson ends.

My child lacks confidence – can you help?

Absolutely. Many students join GG Maths feeling uncertain about their abilities. We focus on building confidence step by step, celebrating progress, and creating a safe environment where mistakes are part of learning. Over time, students go from saying “I can’t do maths” to “I can do this.”

How are tutors trained?

Every GG Maths tutor is carefully selected and personally trained by Giuliano. They all follow our proven, systematic approach while also bringing their own strengths and personality to lessons. Ongoing support ensures consistency across the team.

Learning Support & Resources

What’s included in your learning portal?

Our portal gives students access to past exam questions sorted by topic and difficulty, video lessons for tricky topics, challenge problems, homework tasks, and more. It’s designed to make revision focused, effective, and stress-free.

How do students revise outside of lessons?

Students can rewatch topic videos, practise questions from our database, or message their tutor if they get stuck. We also offer optional homework with personalised marking and feedback.

Do parents get updates on progress?

Yes! After every lesson, we send clear, personalised notes outlining what was covered, key takeaways, and suggested next steps. You’ll also receive monthly progress reports and have the option to book regular check-ins with Giuliano.

What if my child is aiming for top universities?

We offer advanced materials including Olympiad and STEP-style problems for high-performing students preparing for competitive courses like Maths, Engineering, and Physics at top universities.

Practical Details

How long are lessons?

Most lessons are 60 minutes, but we also offer longer sessions for A-Level or intensive revision.

How much do lessons cost?

Our lesson rates range from £35 to £65 per hour, depending on the tutor’s experience and the level being taught. We’ll discuss the best fit for your child during your free consultation and recommend a plan that works for your goals and budget.

What’s your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. Frequent late cancellations may be charged.

How do we get started?

Simply book a free consultation. We’ll discuss your goals, assess your child’s needs, and recommend a personalised plan.

General Questions

Are your tutors DBS checked?

Yes, all of our tutors are fully DBS checked and carefully vetted. You can trust that your child is working with a qualified, responsible professional.

What if my child isn’t used to online lessons?

Our lessons are designed to be engaging and interactive, even for students new to online learning. Most students quickly adapt and often prefer the flexibility and resources that come with online tuition.

Do you follow the school curriculum?

Yes – we align every lesson with your child’s specific exam board and school curriculum. We also adapt to any homework, tests, or areas they’re struggling with in school.

How involved do I need to be as a parent?

We’ll handle the tutoring, but we keep you in the loop. You’ll receive lesson notes, progress reports, and the option to schedule review calls so you always know how your child is doing.

What if we need to pause lessons during holidays?

No problem! Just let us know in advance – we’re flexible and can resume whenever you're ready. Many students even find holiday lessons a great chance to catch up or get ahead.

Will my child have the same tutor every time?

Yes, students are matched with one dedicated tutor for consistency and relationship-building. This allows the tutor to really understand their learning style and progress.
